# Automora

> **Less Manual Work. More Momentum.**

Automora is an intelligent automation company building the infrastructure layer for the next generation of business operations. We design, deploy and maintain self-hosted automation systems that give businesses full ownership of their data, their workflows, and their AI.

---

## What We Build

We are building a suite of tools and services that sit at the intersection of **business automation**, **personal AI**, and **decentralised finance**.

### Business Automation
End-to-end automation solutions for businesses who want to eliminate repetitive manual work without sacrificing control or privacy. Every solution we deploy runs on dedicated, self-hosted infrastructure — no third-party cloud dependencies, no data leaving your environment.

### Self-Hosted Infrastructure Stack
Our entire product suite is built on a hardened self-hosted stack. This means:

- **Full data sovereignty** — client data never touches external AI training pipelines
- **Always on** — 24/7 uptime monitored and maintained
- **Secure by design** — zero trust networking, encrypted tunnels, no exposed ports
- **Scalable** — built to grow with the businesses we serve

### Mora — Personal Sovereign AI *(In Development)*
Mora is our flagship product. A fully autonomous personal AI that runs on dedicated home or office hardware, knows your business inside out, and acts on your behalf. Mora is not a chatbot. She is an operational intelligence layer that reads, thinks, and executes.

Mora's capabilities include:
- Long-term memory via vector embeddings
- Direct integration with business systems and data sources
- Autonomous task execution with human-in-the-loop approval
- Local model inference — no API dependency, no usage limits
- Voice interaction, image and video generation
- Smart environment control via Home Assistant integration

Mora is the product. Automora is the company that builds and supports her.
